The ultimate headlamp for sports outdoors and outdoor recreation, engineered for performance and reliability in any condition.

The Black Diamond Equipment Sprinter 500 delivers 500 lumen output, illuminated by powerful LED technology that ensures you see clearly in even the darkest conditions. Whether you're on a camping trip or trekking through rugged terrain, the Sprinter 500 is a trusted headlamp that enhances safety and visibility. Its rugged build and advanced features make it perfect for hiking, camping, and any outdoor activity that demands dependable lighting.
