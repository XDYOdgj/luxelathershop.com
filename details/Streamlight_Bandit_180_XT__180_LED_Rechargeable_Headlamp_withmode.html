Streamlight Bandit 180 XT

The Streamlight Bandit 180 XT is a versatile and powerful headlamp that combines portability with top-tier performance. With adjustable modes including Hi, Low, and Emergency Flash, you can customize the light to fit your specific needs, whether you're hiking through the woods, camping in the mountains, or tackling any other adventure.

Key Features

  • Rechargeable USB headlamp with 180-lumen output

  • Slim and lightweight design for easy handling
  • IPX4 weather resistance and 2-meter impact resistance for durability

Built with heavy-duty construction and high-quality materials, the Bandit 180 XT is built to last.
